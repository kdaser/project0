The power of connected hotel systems

The primary hotel platform is the reservation system, made up of usually a CRS, or "Central Reservation System", and a PMS, or "Property Management System". The first is generally the retailing platform, which holds rates and room availability so they can be sold to people looking for hotel rooms on connected "channels", and the second, the PMS, is the operations management tool which supports functions such as check-in, check-out, folio printing and housekeeping.
